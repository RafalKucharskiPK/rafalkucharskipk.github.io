people

Meet Rafał Kucharski and his dynamic research group at Jagiellonian University in Kraków. From Principal Investigators to students, this team engages in groundbreaking projects on transport systems, machine learning, and urban mobility. Discover their members, achievements, and ongoing contributions to advancing these fields.

PI

Rafał Kucharski

I am an Associate Professor at the Faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science, Jagiellonian University in Krakow (Poland) with the Group of Machine Learning Research GMUM. I run the ERC Starting Grant COeXISTENCE where I bridge between ML and transportation - we simulate how intelligent machines compete with humans for limited urban resources (space) and what can we expect. I am also involved in Horizon Europe project, SUM. From 2021 to 2024 I led the NCN Opus Grant on Shared Mobility in the pandemic times.
