Last Tuesday during our group seminar, we hosted [Ye Li](https://www.rafalkucharskilab.pl/research/Ye_Li/) — an assistant professor from Beijing University of Technology&nbsp;currently visiting Jagiellonian University for three months through a collaboration supported by the _European Research Council_ and _National Natural Science Foundation of China_.

Ye specializes in Macroscopic Fundamental Diagram (MFD) research, and together we are investigating new approaches to routing autonomous vehicle fleets within MFD frameworks. The methodology looks very promising, and we hope to share results soon. 👌
