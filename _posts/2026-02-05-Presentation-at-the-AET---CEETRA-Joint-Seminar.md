Today, [Rafał](https://www.rafalkucharskilab.pl/research/rafal_kucharski/) presents his recent research at the joint seminar of the [**Association for European Transport (AET)**](https://www.linkedin.com/company/aetransport/) and the [**Central European Excellence in Transportation Research Association (CEETRA)**](https://www.linkedin.com/company/ceetra-association/posts/?feedView=all).

In his talk, Rafał will introduce the concept of Post-Wardropian Urban Mobility Games with Connected Autonomous Vehicles, which summarizes key findings from our ERC [**COeXISTENCE**](https://www.rafalkucharskilab.pl/research/coexistence/) project. 

The session will be co-presented with [Roxani Gkavra](https://www.linkedin.com/in/roxanigkavra/?lipi=urn%3Ali%3Apage%3Ad_flagship3_profile_view_base_recent_activity_content_view%3Biu4h6t7cQJ%2BSUtE%2Fzpv%2BKA%3D%3D) and moderated by [Jacek Pawlak](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jacek-pawlak/).
