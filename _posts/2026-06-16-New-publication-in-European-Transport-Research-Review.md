Launching a new mobility service is challenging when future demand is unknown. [Our new study](https://rafalkucharskipk.github.io/assets/img/on-demand-graphics.webp) presents a practical framework for identifying the best locations for on-demand pooled transit feeders before deployment. Tested in 12 areas of Kraków, the approach helps cities prioritize pilot projects and make more informed investment decisions in shared mobility.

The full paper is available as an open-access publication [here](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1186/s12544-026-00801-9?utm_source=rct_congratemailt&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=oa_20260616&utm_content=10.1186/s12544-026-00801-9)
